Who we are 

UO STRUCTIQUE SDN BHD is a total communications infrastructure solutions provider that provides innovative products and services to satisfy the needs of the cellular, fixed and broadband networks. Our extensive portfolio encompasses a wide array of offerings, including self-supporting structures, civil, electrical, and mechanical solutions, as well as cutting-edge fiber optics technology. UO STRUCTIQUE SDN BHD is a fast-growing organization with the ability to plan, design, deploy, develop, integrate, maintain, manage, monitor, support, and optimize communication systems across Malaysia.

Our products and services are supported by a range of high-quality support services, ensuring reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction at every step of the communication infrastructure lifecycle.

Project Services

We offer complete project services, from planning and design to deployment and maintenance, ensuring smooth execution with advanced solutions.

Permitting & Liaison

After site acquisition, UO STRUCTIQUE SDN BHD handles project management, consultancy, and permitting by preparing submissions, consulting authorities, managing feedback, and securing approvals.

Tower Engineering

Ensures telecom towers stay in top condition through routine maintenance using TPM and best practices. Pre-TPM data includes engineering analysis, site location, layout plans, and other relevant drawings.

Fiber & Wayleave

Verify and confirm the marking and pegging of ROW coordinates and land lot numbers by referring to the District Land Office. Ensuring it is endorsed by a certified Land Surveyor or Utility Surveyor. 

Site Acquisition

Site acquisition services by researching, analyzing, and negotiating lease contracts, along with zoning wireless telecommunications sites. These services help telecommunications stakeholders identify strategic locations.

Permitting Agriculture

The goal is to ensure that all land used by vegetable operators complies with Act 172 Section 21 (Planning Permission), Act 133 (Land Works), and Act 127 (EIA). To regulate land development to prevent risks to local residents' safety and the environment.

CME Construction

Oversee the process from preliminary design to the implementation of civil, mechanical, and electrical aspects. This includes setting up infrastructure for installing radio transmission systems that transmit and receive signals.

Soil Investigation

Soil investigation, or geotechnical investigation, determines the soil layers and properties beneath a site. UO STRUCTIQUE SDN BHD conducts Probe Mackintosh or Bore Hole activities as needed by the client.

Land Survey

Cadastral surveys support engineering surveys and must match boundary marks and triangulation points set by the Survey Department. Land surveys guide consultants and contractors in telco development, like access and slope.

Underground Survey 

UDM maps underground utilities like pipelines and cables using GPR, RFL, and GPS. It helps safely install new pipelines or cables, tracks utilities along road reserves (ROW), and creates As-Built plans for new installations.

Corporate Business

The corporate team offers support and manages various systems and processes, including travel and expense claims, timesheets, organizational changes, training, compliance, offsite events, and general office planning.

Data Intelligence & Asset Management

We integrate and centralize GIS data (e.g., SI, LS, UDM, designs, fleet) into our TechnoGIS Quantum Lab to improve result quality and speed up the project timeline.

Smart City Consultancy

We create customized smart city solutions for local councils or developers, advising on business models, revenue, and contracts. Additionally, we connect investors with councils for smart city development projects.
